How these numbers are calculated

Leads lost to slow response: research shows a 6-hour response time loses roughly 15 percent of leads to faster competitors. This scales with your response time.
Conversion uplift: responding in under 60 seconds typically lifts conversion by 2 to 5 percent. We model a conservative 3.5 percent at the default settings.
Staff hours: automation handles reminders, follow-ups, and data entry. We assume 90 percent reduction in manual follow-up hours.
Annual impact: combines 12 months of recovered revenue from faster response plus 12 months of staff cost savings from removed manual work.
These are estimates based on industry benchmarks. Your actual results will depend on your specific business, team, and industry.
