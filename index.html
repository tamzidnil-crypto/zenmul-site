Case Study: SalDev

From 4-day delays to matched within the hour

SalDev, a specialist cargo and freight forwarding operation, was matching containers to customers manually. It took 1 to 4 days, caused missed opportunities, and consumed hours of team time every week.

We built a suite of five automation systems: batch Excel ingestion from agent emails, an AI-powered container matching engine running hourly, a daily exception and delay digest, and an automated customer notification flow.

FAQ

Common questions

Do I need any technical knowledge? +
None at all. You tell us what you want to stop doing manually, we handle everything else: the design, the build, the testing, and the maintenance. You just use the result.
How long does it take to go live? +
Most systems are live within 5 to 10 business days of the audit. Complex multi-workflow systems take a little longer, but we always agree on a timeline upfront.
What tools do you use? +
We build primarily on n8n with AI for language tasks, connected to Gmail, Google Sheets, Telegram, and any other tools your business already uses. No new software to learn or subscribe to.
What if the automation breaks or needs changing? +
We monitor and maintain everything. If something breaks or your process changes, we handle it. Maintenance is included in all ongoing plans.
