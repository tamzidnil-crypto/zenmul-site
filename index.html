FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need any technical knowledge to use the automations?
+

No. We build everything and document how it works in plain English. You interact with your existing tools the same way you always have. The automation runs invisibly in the background.

What happens if the automation breaks?
+

n8n has built-in error handling and logging. If a workflow fails, it logs exactly which step and why. On support plans we monitor this for you.

Will I be locked into your platform if I stop working with you?
+

No. We build on n8n (open source) and Google Workspace tools you already own. All workflows, credentials, and documentation belong to you. If we disappeared tomorrow, your systems keep running.

How long does it take to go live?
+

Most projects are live in 5–10 working days after sign-off on the design. Larger suites can take 2–3 weeks. We give you a clear timeline in the audit phase.

What's included in a free audit?
+

A 30-minute call where we map your current manual processes, identify the highest-leverage automations, and give you a no-obligation scope and rough quote. No payment card, no commitment.
