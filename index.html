The Problem

Finding decision-makers at startups actively hiring engineers meant hours of manual scraping across job boards, cross-referencing LinkedIn, and hunting for verified emails, every single day.

What we built
  • Scrapes three startup job boards (a16z, Y Combinator, Wellfound) for fresh engineering postings.
  • Identifies the decision-maker via Apollo: founders, CTOs, heads of engineering.
  • Verifies every email through Clearout, protecting sender reputation.
  • Delivers leads to a live Google Sheet, deduplicated and formatted.
  • Sends a daily Telegram summary.
The Result

A hands-off system that turns three job boards into a verified, decision-maker-level lead list every morning at 8AM. Zero manual work. What used to be a daily research grind is now a notification on the client's phone.
