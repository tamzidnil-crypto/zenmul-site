How it works

From manual mess to a
workflow that just runs.

Every Zenmul engagement follows the same four-step process. No surprises, no handoffs, no black boxes. You see exactly what's being built and why at every stage.

01
Week 1

Audit

We start by mapping the repetitive work eating your team's week. Not just what you think you want automated, but what actually costs you the most time and money when done manually.

  • A detailed walkthrough of your current manual processes
  • Identification of the highest-ROI automation opportunities
  • An honest assessment of what's worth building and what isn't
  • Examples from your vertical: a freight operator gets a Cargo Brain audit, a clinic gets an enquiry and no-show audit, a recruiter gets a candidate pipeline audit
  • A prioritised roadmap you can act on immediately, whether with us or not
02
Week 1-2

Map & design

Before writing a single node, we design the workflow end to end. Every trigger, every branch, every tool it has to touch, every failure state. You approve the design before we build anything.

  • Full workflow diagram showing every step and decision point
  • Tool and integration selection based on your existing stack
  • Edge case planning: what happens when data is missing, late, or wrong
  • Clear scope: exactly what the workflow does and doesn't do
03
Week 2-3

Build & test

We build the workflow in n8n on your own self-hosted instance. Every node is tested against your real data, not sample data. You review before anything goes live.

  • Built node by node in n8n on Cloud Infrastructure or your own infrastructure
  • Tested with your actual data and real-world edge cases
  • Error handling and fallback logic built in from the start
  • You review the live workflow before final deployment
04
Ongoing

Run & support

Once live, the workflow runs 24/7. You get monitoring alerts if anything fails. We handle fixes. As your business changes, we iterate on the workflow to match.

  • 24/7 automated monitoring with instant failure alerts
  • Fast fixes — most issues resolved within one business day
  • Ongoing iteration as your processes evolve
  • Monthly review on Full Suite and Automation Partner plans
The stack

Tools we build with

Every tool below is actively used across Zenmul client builds. We select the best tool for each job — not the most expensive, not the most well-known. Each one earns its place.

Core Infrastructure

n8n

Self-hosted workflow automation engine. 400+ integrations, zero per-task fees, open source. You own everything we build inside it.

AI Language Model

Powers every intelligent task — email drafting, lead scoring, intent classification, document generation. We select the best model for each use case.

Self-Hosted Server

Your n8n runs on your own infrastructure. Always on, automatic restarts, zero vendor dependency. Your data never leaves your environment.

Communication and Data

Gmail

Automated email sending for confirmations, reminders, follow-ups, and onboarding sequences across every vertical.

Telegram

Instant digest alerts, exception notifications, and daily summaries delivered to your phone the moment they matter.

Google Sheets

The operational data layer across all client builds. Triggers, logs, and updates live in sheets your team already uses.

Google Drive

File storage and document management. Workflows read, write, and organise files automatically without manual uploads.

Google Forms

Intake forms that trigger workflows automatically. Enquiries, audits, onboarding — captured and processed in seconds.

Google Calendar

Appointment scheduling and reminder triggers. Syncs with booking flows to send confirmations and reminders automatically.

Lead Generation and Enrichment

Apollo.io

B2B lead database and enrichment. Finds verified contact data for decision-makers across industries and company sizes.

Clearout

Email verification before any outreach is sent. Eliminates bounces, protects sender reputation, improves deliverability.

Apify

Web scraping and data extraction. Pulls leads from directories, funding databases, job boards, and company websites at scale.

WhoisFreaks

Domain WHOIS lookups and expiry monitoring. Powers the domain watchlist automation that alerts on expiring opportunities.

REST APIs

Custom HTTP integrations with any platform that has an API. If it has an endpoint, it can be automated.

Webhooks

Real-time event triggers from any external platform. Form submissions, payment events, CRM updates — caught instantly.
