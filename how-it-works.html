How it works

From manual mess to a
workflow that just runs.

Every Zenmul engagement follows the same four-step process. No surprises, no handoffs, no black boxes. You see exactly what's being built and why at every stage.

01
Week 1

Audit

We start by mapping the repetitive work eating your team's week. Not just what you think you want automated, but what actually costs you the most time and money when done manually.

  • A detailed walkthrough of your current manual processes
  • Identification of the highest-ROI automation opportunities
  • An honest assessment of what's worth building and what isn't
  • Examples from your vertical: a freight operator gets a Cargo Brain audit, a clinic gets an enquiry and no-show audit, a recruiter gets a candidate pipeline audit
  • A prioritised roadmap you can act on immediately, whether with us or not
02
Week 1-2

Map & design

Before writing a single node, we design the workflow end to end. Every trigger, every branch, every tool it has to touch, every failure state. You approve the design before we build anything.

  • Full workflow diagram showing every step and decision point
  • Tool and integration selection based on your existing stack
  • Edge case planning: what happens when data is missing, late, or wrong
  • Clear scope: exactly what the workflow does and doesn't do
03
Week 2-3

Build & test

We build the workflow in n8n on your own self-hosted instance. Every node is tested against your real data, not sample data. You review before anything goes live.

  • Built node by node in n8n on Railway or your own infrastructure
  • Tested with your actual data and real-world edge cases
  • Error handling and fallback logic built in from the start
  • You review the live workflow before final deployment
04
Ongoing

Run & support

Once live, the workflow runs 24/7. You get monitoring alerts if anything fails. We handle fixes. As your business changes, we iterate on the workflow to match.

  • 24/7 automated monitoring with instant failure alerts
  • Fast fixes — most issues resolved within one business day
  • Ongoing iteration as your processes evolve
  • Monthly review on Full Suite and Automation Partner plans
The stack

Tools we build with

n8n

Self-hosted workflow automation. Open source, 400+ integrations, zero per-task fees. You own everything we build in it.

Groq AI

Fast, cheap inference for Llama 3.3 70B. Powers every AI decision in your workflows, from lead scoring to intent classification.

Google Workspace

Sheets, Gmail, Drive, Forms. The data lives where your team already works, no new dashboards to learn.

Telegram

Instant alerts and summaries sent directly to your phone. No logging into dashboards to know if the workflow ran.

Apollo + Clearout

Lead enrichment and email verification. Finds the decision-maker and confirms the email before anything is sent.

Railway

Reliable hosting for your self-hosted n8n instance. Always on, always running, no manual server management.
